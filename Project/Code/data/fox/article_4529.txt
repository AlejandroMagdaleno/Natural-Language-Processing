Washington D.C. Mayor Muriel Bowser, a vocal DC statehood backer, is slated to testify along with Marc Morial, president and CEO of the National Urban League, as well as several legal experts.

Republicans have widely panned the DC statehood effort as a Democratic power play to expand the Senate with representatives from a liberal stronghold and have questioned the constitutionality of making the federal district into a state.
