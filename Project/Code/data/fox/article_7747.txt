Winning for Women, the Republican PAC dedicated to electing 
women
, released its first round of endorsements for the 2022 midterm elections Tuesday, telling Fox News the &#34;road to the majority runs through&#34; these candidates&#39; districts, while vowing to make the next cycle &#34;another record year for Republican women.&#34;
Winning for Women PAC shared its first endorsements with Fox News Tuesday. The list includes nine freshman House women.
13 GOP WOMEN JOIN HOUSE, MAKE HISTORY
Winning for Women endorsed GOP Reps. Young Kim of California, Michelle Steel of California, Maria Elvira Salazar of Florida, Ashley Hinson of Iowa, Mariannette Miller-Meeks of Iowa, Nicole Malliotakis of New York, Stephanie Bice of Oklahoma, Nancy Mace of South Carolina, and Beth Van Duyne of Texas.
The super PAC told Fox News it would max out donations to the campaigns of each candidate.
&#34;The road to the majority runs through the districts of these congresswoman,&#34; Winning for Women spokeswoman Olivia Perez-Cubas told Fox News. &#34;As business owners, journalists and doctors, each of these women has given a real voice to their constituents.&#34;
She added: &#34;Despite efforts by the left to unseat them, we&#39;re sending a message loud and clear: Republican women are here to stay.&#34;
Perez-Cubas also told Fox News that they are looking forward to &#34;working together to make 2022 another record year for Republican women.&#34;
&#34;After running a small business, having a career, raising a family and now serving the community I&#39;ve called home for decades in Congress, I believe the American dream is alive and real, and that we need more women&#39;s voices at the table to move our country forward,&#34; Kim, the first Korean American woman to serve in Congress, told Fox News. &#34;Winning For Women has encouraged me and so many other conservative women to run for office and advance free market principles since day one, and I&#39;m grateful to have their endorsement.&#34;
