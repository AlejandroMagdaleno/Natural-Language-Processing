The G-7 summit is underway in the 
United Kingdom

The leaders of the Group of Seven economies are meeting in Cornwall, in the southwest of England, for the summit, which runs from Friday to Sunday.
Key talking points will include the 
coronavirus
, climate, Russia, China, and the Middle East. The global economy is also expected to take center stage, according to a White House statement.
